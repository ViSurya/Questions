Write the four features of modernization.

Modernization is characterized by four key features:

  1. Secularization: The separation of religion from the state and other aspects of public life.

  2. Industrialization: The shift from an agrarian to an industrial economy, leading to increased urbanization and technological advancements.

  3. Rationalization: The application of reason and logic to various aspects of life, including politics, economics, and social relations.

  4. Differentiation: The increasing specialization and division of labor in society, leading to the emergence of distinct social groups and institutions.
