What is rectifier ? Name different kinds of rectifier. Describe the pn junction diode as an half wave rectifier.

A rectifier is an electronic device that converts alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). There are two main types of rectifiers: half-wave rectifiers and full-wave rectifiers. A pn junction diode can be used as a half-wave rectifier. In a half-wave rectifier, only one half of the AC waveform is allowed to pass through the diode, while the other half is blocked. This results in a DC output that is pulsating in nature.
