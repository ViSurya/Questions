What made the peddler finally change his ways?

The peddler's encounter with the young girl, Edla, marked a turning point in his life. Her kindness and innocence touched his heart, making him realize the emptiness of his deceitful ways. Edla's unwavering trust in him, despite his initial dishonesty, awakened a sense of guilt and remorse within him. This encounter became a catalyst for his transformation, leading him to abandon his life of deception and embrace honesty and integrity.
