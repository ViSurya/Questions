Prove that sin^(-1)〖3/5-sin^(-1) 8/17=sin^(-1) 13/85〗.

Let θ = sin-1(3/5) and φ = sin-1(8/17). Then, sin θ = 3/5 and sin φ = 8/17. Using the identity sin(α - β) = sin α cos β - cos α sin β, we have:

sin(θ - φ) = sin θ cos φ - cos θ sin φ = (3/5)(17/√(172 - 82)) - (4/5)(8/√(172 - 82)) = 13/√(172 - 82).

Therefore, θ - φ = sin-1(13/√(172 - 82)) = sin-1(13/85).
