Write any four main regional aspirations of the North-East States.

The North-Eastern states of India have several regional aspirations, including:

  1. Economic Development:

    The region seeks accelerated economic development, including infrastructure improvement, industrialization, and job creation.

  2. Cultural Preservation:

    The states aim to preserve and promote their unique cultural heritage, languages, and traditions.

  3. Political Autonomy:

    Some states desire greater political autonomy within the Indian Union, including increased decision-making powers.

  4. Improved Connectivity:

    The region seeks better transportation and communication infrastructure to enhance connectivity with the rest of India.
