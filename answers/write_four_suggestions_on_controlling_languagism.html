Write four suggestions on controlling languagism.

To control languagism, several measures can be taken:

  1. Promote multilingualism in education and public life, allowing individuals to use their native languages while also learning other languages.
  2. Encourage cultural exchange and understanding among different linguistic groups, fostering respect and appreciation for diverse languages and cultures.
  3. Implement anti-discrimination laws and policies that protect individuals from discrimination based on language, ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  4. Raise awareness about the negative consequences of languagism, educating the public about the importance of linguistic diversity and the rights of linguistic minorities.
