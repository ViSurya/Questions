Fill in the blanks choosing the correct option : (i) I have seen _______ lot of places. (a) all (b) a (c) an (d) the

The correct option is (b) a. The sentence is "I have seen a lot of places." The word "a" is used before a singular noun to indicate one of a kind. In this case, "a lot" is a singular noun, so we use "a" before it.
