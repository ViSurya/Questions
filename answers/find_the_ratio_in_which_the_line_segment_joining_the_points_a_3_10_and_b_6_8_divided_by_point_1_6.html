Find the ratio in which the line segment joining the points A (-3, 10) and B (6, - 8) divided by point (-1, 6).

Let the ratio in which the line segment joining the points A(-3, 10) and B(6, -8) is divided by the point P(-1, 6) be k:1. Then, the coordinates of P are given by the section formula:

x-coordinate of P -1 = (k * 6 - 1 * 3) / (k + 1)
y-coordinate of P 6 = (k * (-8) - 1 * 10) / (k + 1)

Solving these equations, we get k = 2. Therefore, the ratio in which the line segment AB is divided by the point P is 2:1.
