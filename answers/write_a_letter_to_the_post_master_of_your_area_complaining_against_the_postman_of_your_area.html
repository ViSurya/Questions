Write a letter to the Post Master of your area complaining against the postman of your area.

To,
The Post Master, [Your Area]

Subject: Complaint against the Postman of [Your Area]

Respected Sir/Madam,
I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the services of the postman in our area. As a resident of [Your Area], I have been experiencing consistent issues with the delivery of my mail and packages.

The postman, [Postman's Name], has been negligent in his duties, resulting in delayed or lost mail. Despite repeated complaints to the local post office, the situation has not improved. I have also noticed that the postman often handles mail roughly, leading to damaged or torn envelopes.

I kindly request your immediate attention to this matter. I believe that the postman's behavior is unacceptable and needs to be addressed promptly. I would appreciate it if you could take appropriate action to ensure that the mail delivery services in our area are improved and that the postman is held accountable for his actions.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely, [Your Name]
