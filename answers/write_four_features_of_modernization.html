Write four features of modernization.

Modernization is characterized by four key features:

  1. Technological Advancement: The introduction and widespread adoption of new technologies, such as machinery, automation, and digital communication, leading to increased productivity and efficiency.

  2. Urbanization: The movement of people from rural areas to urban centers, resulting in the growth of cities and the emergence of new social and economic patterns.

  3. Secularization: The decline of religious influence in various aspects of public life, leading to a greater separation between religion and politics, education, and other societal institutions.

  4. Increased Social Mobility: The breaking down of traditional social hierarchies and the creation of opportunities for individuals to move up the social ladder based on merit and achievement rather than birth or social status.
