Write three consequences of Emergency.

The Emergency in India, imposed from 1975 to 1977, had several consequences:

  1. Suspension of Civil Liberties:

    The Emergency led to the suspension of fundamental rights, including the right to freedom of speech, assembly, and movement.

  2. Political Repression:

    Thousands of political opponents, including leaders and activists, were arrested and detained without trial.

  3. Economic Disruption:

    The Emergency caused economic disruption, leading to shortages of essential commodities and a decline in industrial production.
