If A=[(3 √3 2,4 2 0)], then show that (A')' = A.

Given A = [(3√3 2, 4 2 0)], the transpose of A, denoted as A', is obtained by interchanging the rows and columns of A. Therefore, A' = [(3√3 4), (2 2 0)]. Taking the transpose of A' again, we get (A')' = [(3√3 2), (4 2 0)] = A. Hence, it is proven that (A')' = A.
