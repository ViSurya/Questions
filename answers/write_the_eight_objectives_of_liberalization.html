Write the Eight objectives of liberalization,

The eight objectives of liberalization are:

  1. To promote economic growth and development by removing restrictions on trade and investment.
  2. To increase competition and efficiency in the economy.
  3. To attract foreign investment and technology.
  4. To promote exports and reduce imports.
  5. To reduce government intervention in the economy.
  6. To create a more level playing field for businesses.
  7. To promote economic freedom and choice.
  8. To improve the standard of living for all citizens.
