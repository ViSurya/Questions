How population growth affects the economic development of the country ? Write. (any three)

Population growth can significantly impact a country's economic development in various ways:

  1. Increased Labor Force: A larger population can provide a larger labor force, potentially boosting production and economic growth.
  2. Expanded Market: A growing population creates a larger domestic market, stimulating demand for goods and services and encouraging businesses to invest and expand.
  3. Resource Strain: Rapid population growth can strain resources such as food, water, and energy, leading to shortages and increased costs, which can hinder economic development.
