Explain following: (a) Ligand (b) Coordination number.

(a) Ligand: A ligand is a molecule, ion, or atom that has at least one atom or ion capable of donating a pair of electrons to the metal center to form a coordinate bond.

(b) Coordination number: The coordination number of a metal ion is the number of ligand molecules, ions, or atoms that are bonded to the metal ion in a complex.
