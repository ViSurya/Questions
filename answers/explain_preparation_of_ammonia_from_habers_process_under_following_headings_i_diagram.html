The Haber process is an industrial method for the production of ammonia from hydrogen and nitrogen gases. It is a crucial process in the production of fertilizers and other nitrogen-based chemicals.

Step Reaction Conditions
1 N2 + 3H2 → 2NH3 200-300 atm pressure, 450-500°C temperature, iron oxide catalyst
