The side of a square sheet of metal is increasing at the rate of 5 cm/minute. At what rate its area is increasing when the side is 20 cm long?

When the side of a square sheet of metal increases at a constant rate, the rate of change of its area is equal to the perimeter of the square multiplied by the rate of change of its side.

Therefore, if the side of the square is increasing at a rate of 5 cm/minute and the side is 20 cm long, the rate of change of its area is 4 * 20 * 5 = 400 square centimeters per minute.
