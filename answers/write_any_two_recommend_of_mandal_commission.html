Write any two recommend of Mandal Commission.

The Mandal Commission, also known as the Second Backward Classes Commission, was established in 1979 to identify and recommend measures for the advancement of socially and educationally backward classes in India. Two of its key recommendations were:

  1. Reservation of 27% of seats in government jobs and educational institutions for Other Backward Classes (OBCs).
  2. Provision of scholarships, hostels, and other facilities for OBC students to promote their educational and economic development.
