Explain mole fraction.

Mole fraction is a measure of the concentration of a component in a mixture. It is defined as the ratio of the number of moles of a component to the total number of moles of all components in the mixture. Mole fraction is a dimensionless quantity and its value ranges from 0 to 1. A mole fraction of 0 indicates that the component is not present in the mixture, while a mole fraction of 1 indicates that the component is the only component present.
