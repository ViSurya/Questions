A metallic sphere of radius 4.2 cm is melted and recast into the shape of a cylinder of radius 6 cm. Find the height of the cylinder.

The volume of the sphere is equal to the volume of the cylinder. Using this, we can find the height of the cylinder.

Volume of sphere = Volume of cylinder

$$frac{4}{3}pi(4.2)^3 = pi(6)^2h$$

Solving for h, we get h = 3.5 cm.
