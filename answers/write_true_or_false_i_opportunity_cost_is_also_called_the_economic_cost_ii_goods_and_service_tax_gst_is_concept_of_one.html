Write True or False : (i) Opportunity cost is also called the economic cost. (ii) Goods and Service Tax (GST) is concept of One

True. Opportunity cost is the value of the next best alternative that is given up when a choice is made. It is also known as the economic cost because it represents the real cost of a decision in terms of the opportunities that are foregone.
True. Goods and Service Tax (GST) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the manufacture, sale, and consumption of goods and services in India. It is a single tax that replaces multiple indirect taxes levied by the central and state governments.
