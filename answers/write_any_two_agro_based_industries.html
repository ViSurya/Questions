Write any two agro based industries.

Agro-based industries are those that use agricultural products as their primary raw materials. Two examples of agro-based industries are:

  1. Sugar Industry: This industry uses sugarcane as its primary raw material to produce sugar, molasses, and other by-products.
  2. Textile Industry: This industry uses cotton, jute, and other natural fibers as its primary raw materials to produce fabrics, yarns, and other textile products.
