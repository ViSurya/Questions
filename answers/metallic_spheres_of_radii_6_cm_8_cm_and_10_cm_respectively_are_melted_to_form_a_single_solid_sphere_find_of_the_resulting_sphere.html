Metallic spheres of radii 6 cm, 8 cm and 10 cm respectively, are melted to form a single solid sphere. Find of the resulting sphere.

Let the radius of the resulting sphere be R. The volume of the resulting sphere is equal to the sum of the volumes of the three spheres melted to form it.

Using the formula for the volume of a sphere, we get:

$$ \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3 = \frac{4}{3}\pi (6^3 + 8^3 + 10^3) $$

Solving for R, we get:

$$ R = \sqrt[3]{6^3 + 8^3 + 10^3} = 9.24 \text{ cm} $$
