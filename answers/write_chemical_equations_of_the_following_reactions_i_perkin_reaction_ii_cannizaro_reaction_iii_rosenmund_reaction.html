Write chemical equations of the following reactions : (i) Perkin reaction (ii) Cannizaro reaction (iii) Rosenmund reaction

Perkin reaction: Benzaldehyde and an aliphatic anhydride react in the presence of sodium acetate to form a cinnamic acid derivative.

Cannizzaro reaction: Aldehydes without an alpha-hydrogen atom, when treated with concentrated alkali, undergo self-oxidation and reduction to form a mixture of alcohol and carboxylic acid.

Rosenmund reaction: Acyl chlorides are reduced to aldehydes using hydrogen and a palladium catalyst poisoned with sulfur or quinoline.
