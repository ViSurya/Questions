Mahajan Ltd. issued 30,000 equity shares of Rs. 10 each to the public, receiving the entire amount in a single payment. This transaction resulted in an inflow of capital, increasing the company's equity share capital by Rs. 3,00,000 (30,000 shares x Rs. 10 per share).
