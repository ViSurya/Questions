What is meant by logic gates ? What are OR and AND gates ? Draw their symbols and electrical equivalent circuits.

Logic gates are electronic circuits that perform basic logical operations like AND, OR, and NOT on one or more input signals to produce a single output signal.

OR gate produces a HIGH output if any of its inputs is HIGH, while AND gate produces a HIGH output only if all of its inputs are HIGH.
