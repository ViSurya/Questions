Write a letter to the Superintendent of Police of your district for a ban on the free use of loudspeakers during examination.

To,
The Superintendent of Police, [District Name], [State Name].

Subject: Request for a Ban on the Free Use of Loudspeakers During Examinations.

Respected Sir/Madam,
I am writing to bring to your attention the issue of the free use of loudspeakers during examinations, which has become a significant concern for students in our district. The noise pollution caused by loudspeakers can be extremely disruptive and distracting, making it difficult for students to concentrate and perform well in their exams.

I kindly request your immediate intervention in this matter. A ban on the free use of loudspeakers during examination hours would create a conducive environment for students to focus and perform to the best of their abilities. This would not only ensure fairness in the examination process but also contribute to the overall well-being and success of our students.

I believe that this measure is essential to safeguard the rights of students and uphold the integrity of the examination process. I urge you to take prompt action to implement a ban on the use of loudspeakers during examination hours in our district.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely, [Your Name] [Class and Section] [School Name]
