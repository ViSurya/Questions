Write the type of pollution.

Pollution can be classified into various types based on its source, nature, and impact on the environment. Some common types of pollution include:

  1. Air Pollution: The contamination of the Earth's atmosphere with harmful or excessive quantities of substances, such as gases, particulate matter, or biological materials, that can cause adverse health effects.
  2. Water Pollution: The contamination of water bodies, such as lakes, rivers, oceans, and groundwater, with harmful substances, including chemicals, pathogens, and waste, which can pose risks to human health and aquatic ecosystems.
  3. Land Pollution: The contamination of the Earth's land surface with harmful substances, such as toxic chemicals, heavy metals, and solid waste, which can affect soil quality, groundwater, and human health.
  4. Noise Pollution: Excessive and unwanted sound that can cause adverse effects on human health, including hearing loss, sleep disturbance, and stress.
  5. Light Pollution: Excessive and inappropriate use of artificial light, which can disrupt natural light cycles, affect wildlife, and cause discomfort and health issues in humans.
