Write any three importance of rail transport.

Rail transport holds immense significance due to its numerous advantages. Firstly, it offers a cost-effective mode of transportation for both passengers and freight, making it accessible to a wider range of individuals and businesses. Secondly, rail networks facilitate seamless connectivity between cities and regions, fostering economic growth and social integration. Lastly, rail transport is generally considered an environmentally friendly option compared to other modes of transportation, contributing to sustainable development.
