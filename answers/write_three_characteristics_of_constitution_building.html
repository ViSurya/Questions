Write three characteristics of constitution building.

Constitution building involves three key characteristics:

  1. Popular Participation: Constitutions are legitimate when they reflect the will of the people. Public participation through debates, discussions, and referendums ensures that the constitution represents the collective aspirations of society.
  2. Consensus Building: Constitution-making requires consensus among various stakeholders, including political parties, interest groups, and civil society organizations. This collaborative approach helps create a constitution that is widely accepted and supported.
  3. Institutional Design: Constitutions establish the framework for government institutions, defining their powers, functions, and relationships. This institutional design aims to ensure checks and balances, prevent tyranny, and protect individual rights and liberties.
