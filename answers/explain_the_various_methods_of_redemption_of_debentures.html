Explain the various methods of redemption of debentures.

Debentures can be redeemed in various ways, including:

  1. Random Redemption: Debentures are redeemed by drawing lots, ensuring a fair and impartial selection process.
  2. Redemption at Par: Debentures are redeemed at their face value, providing investors with the principal amount they initially invested.
  3. Redemption at a Premium: Debentures are redeemed at a price higher than their face value, offering investors a return greater than their initial investment.
  4. Redemption at a Discount: Debentures are redeemed at a price lower than their face value, resulting in a loss for investors compared to their initial investment.
  5. Sinking Fund: A specific fund is created to accumulate funds for the redemption of debentures at maturity or before.
