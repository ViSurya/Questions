Write names of any four human geographers.

Some notable human geographers include:

  1. Yi-Fu Tuan: Known for his work on human-environment interactions and the concept of "topophilia."
  2. David Harvey: A prominent Marxist geographer who has written extensively about capitalism, urbanization, and social justice.
  3. Edward Soja: A cultural geographer who has explored the relationship between space, power, and identity.
  4. Doreen Massey: A feminist geographer who has contributed to the understanding of space, place, and power relations.
