How many are types of computer virus ? Write its disadvantages.

Computer viruses can be categorized into various types based on their behavior and infection methods. Some common types include:

  1. Boot Sector Virus: Infects the boot sector of a storage device, preventing the system from booting properly.
  2. File Infector Virus: Attaches itself to executable files, infecting them and spreading to other files when executed.
  3. Macro Virus: Exploits macros in applications like Microsoft Office to spread through documents and templates.
  4. Resident Virus: Resides in the computer's memory, infecting new files as they are created or accessed.
  5. Polymorphic Virus: Changes its code structure frequently to evade detection by antivirus software.

The disadvantages of computer viruses include:

  • Data Loss: Viruses can corrupt or delete files, leading to data loss and potential financial implications.
  • System Performance Issues: Infected systems may experience slowdowns, crashes, and other performance issues.
  • Security Breaches: Viruses can create backdoors or exploit vulnerabilities, allowing unauthorized access to sensitive information.
  • Network Disruptions: Viruses can spread across networks, infecting multiple computers and disrupting network operations.
  • Legal and Ethical Concerns: Creating and distributing viruses is illegal and unethical, potentially leading to legal consequences.
