Find the area of the shaded region in given figure, where ABCD is a square of side 14 cm.

The area of the shaded region is calculated by subtracting the area of the four quarter circles from the area of the square.

Area of the square = (14 cm)^2 = 196 cm^2

Area of one quarter circle = (1/4) * π * (7 cm)^2 = (49/4)π cm^2

Area of four quarter circles = 4 * (49/4)π cm^2 = 49π cm^2

Area of the shaded region = Area of the square - Area of four quarter circles = 196 cm^2 - 49π cm^2 ≈ 196 cm^2 - 154.47 cm^2 ≈ 41.53 cm^2
