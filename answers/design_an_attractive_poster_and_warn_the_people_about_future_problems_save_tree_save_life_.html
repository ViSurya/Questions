Design an attractive poster and warn the people about future problems Save Tree Save Life .

Trees are the lungs of our planet, providing us with oxygen, food, and shelter. They also help to regulate the climate and protect our water resources. By cutting down trees, we are harming ourselves and our planet.

If we continue to destroy trees at the current rate, we will face a number of serious problems in the future, including:

  1. Increased air pollution, leading to respiratory problems and other health issues.
  2. More extreme weather events, such as droughts, floods, and heat waves.
  3. Loss of biodiversity, as many species of plants and animals rely on trees for survival.

We need to take action now to protect our trees and ensure a healthy future for our planet.
