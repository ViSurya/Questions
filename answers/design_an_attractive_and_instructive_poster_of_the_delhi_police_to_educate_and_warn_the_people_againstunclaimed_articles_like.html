Delhi Police: Unclaimed Articles - A Threat to Safety

Unclaimed articles, such as bags, packages, or electronic devices, can pose a serious threat to public safety. They may contain hazardous materials, explosives, or other dangerous items. If you encounter an unclaimed article, do not touch or move it. Instead, immediately report it to the Delhi Police by calling 100 or visiting your nearest police station.
