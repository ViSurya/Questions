Write four characteristics of Non-Aligned policy.

The Non-Aligned policy, adopted by India, is characterized by four key features:

  1. Independence and Sovereignty: Non-alignment upholds the principles of independence and sovereignty, allowing nations to make decisions without external influence or pressure.
  2. Peaceful Coexistence: It promotes peaceful coexistence among nations, regardless of their political, economic, or social systems, advocating for peaceful resolution of conflicts.
  3. Non-Participation in Military Alliances: Non-aligned countries refrain from joining military alliances or pacts, maintaining neutrality in conflicts between major power blocs.
  4. Economic Cooperation: The policy encourages economic cooperation and development among non-aligned nations, fostering mutual benefits and reducing dependence on external powers.
