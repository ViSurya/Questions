For \(x < 2\), \(f'(x) > 0\), so \(f(x)\) is increasing. For \(x > 2\), \(f'(x) < 0\), so \(f(x)\) is decreasing. Therefore, \(f(x)\) is increasing on the interval \((-\infty, 2)\) and decreasing on the interval \((2, \infty)\).
