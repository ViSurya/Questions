Write any two recommendations of Dar Commission,

The Dar Commission, also known as the States Reorganization Commission, made two significant recommendations:

  1. The reorganization of states should be based on linguistic and cultural homogeneity, aiming to create states with a common language and shared cultural heritage.
  2. The boundaries of states should be redrawn to ensure administrative convenience and economic viability, considering factors such as geographical contiguity, natural boundaries, and economic interdependence.
