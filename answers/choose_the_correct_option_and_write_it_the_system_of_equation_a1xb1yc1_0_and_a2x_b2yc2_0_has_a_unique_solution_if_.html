The system of equations \(a_1x + b_1y + c_1 = 0\) and \(a_2x + b_2y + c_2 = 0\) has a unique solution if the determinant of the coefficient matrix is not equal to zero. The determinant is given by:

Determinant \( \begin{vmatrix} a_1 & b_1 \\ a_2 & b_2 \end{vmatrix} \)

Therefore, the correct option is: \( \begin{vmatrix} a_1 & b_1 \\ a_2 & b_2 \end{vmatrix} \neq 0 \)
