Write any four military organizations of Shivaji.

Shivaji, the great Maratha warrior king, established several military organizations to strengthen his kingdom and defend against enemies. These organizations played a crucial role in his success and the expansion of the Maratha Empire.

  1. Cavalry: Shivaji's cavalry, known as the Mavalas, were highly skilled and mobile light cavalry units. They were instrumental in surprise attacks, raids, and guerrilla warfare.
  2. Infantry: The Maratha infantry, called the Pawans, were well-trained and disciplined foot soldiers. They were equipped with swords, spears, and shields and formed the backbone of Shivaji's armies.
  3. Navy: Shivaji recognized the importance of naval power and established a strong navy. His ships, known as gallivats, were swift and maneuverable, allowing him to control the coastal regions and disrupt enemy supply lines.
  4. Intelligence and Espionage: Shivaji had an extensive network of spies and informants, known as the Bahirjee. They gathered intelligence, infiltrated enemy territories, and provided valuable information for military campaigns.
