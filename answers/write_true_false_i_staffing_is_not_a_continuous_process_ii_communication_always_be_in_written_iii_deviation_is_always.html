(i) False. Staffing is a continuous process involving recruitment, selection, placement, and development of human resources to meet the changing needs of the organization. (ii) False. Communication can be verbal, written, or non-verbal, depending on the situation and the message being conveyed. (iii) False. Deviation is not always negative. It can also be positive when it leads to improved performance or better results.
