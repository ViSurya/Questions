Prove that the sum of three vectors represented by the consecutive sides of triangle is zero vector.

Let a, b, and c be the vectors representing the consecutive sides of a triangle. By the triangle law of vector addition, we have a + b = c. Reversing the direction of c, we get -c = a + b. Adding c to both sides, we obtain a + b + c = 0. Therefore, the sum of the three vectors is the zero vector.
