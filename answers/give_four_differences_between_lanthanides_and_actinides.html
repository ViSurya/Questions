Give four differences between Lanthanides and Actinides.

Lanthanides and Actinides are two series of elements in the periodic table with similar properties. However, there are some key differences between them:

Property Lanthanides Actinides
Atomic Number Range 57 to 71 89 to 103
Electronic Configuration [Xe] 4f1-14 5d0-1 6s2 [Rn] 5f1-14 6d0-1 7s2
Oxidation States +2, +3 +3, +4, +5, +6
Radioactivity All are radioactive All are radioactive, except Actinium and Protactinium
