Under what circumstances can a partner retire from a partnership firm ?

A partner can retire from a partnership firm under various circumstances, including:

  1. Reaching the retirement age specified in the partnership agreement or as per the law.
  2. Illness, incapacity, or other physical or mental disability that prevents the partner from actively participating in the business.
  3. Voluntary retirement due to personal reasons, such as pursuing other interests or relocating to a different city.
  4. Expulsion from the partnership due to misconduct, breach of partnership agreement, or a vote by the majority of the partners.
  5. Death of the partner, which automatically dissolves the partnership unless otherwise agreed upon in the partnership agreement.
