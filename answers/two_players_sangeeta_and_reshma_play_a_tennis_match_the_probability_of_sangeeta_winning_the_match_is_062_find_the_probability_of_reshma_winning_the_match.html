Two players Sangeeta and Reshma play a tennis match. The probability of Sangeeta winning the match is 0.62. Find the probability of Reshma winning the match.

Since the match can only be won by either Sangeeta or Reshma, the sum of probabilities of their victories must be equal to 1. Therefore, the probability of Reshma winning the match is 1 minus the probability of Sangeeta winning.

Probability of Reshma winning = 1 - Probability of Sangeeta winning = 1 - 0.62 = 0.38
