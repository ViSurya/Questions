Write any three features of Cabinet Mission Plan.

The Cabinet Mission Plan, proposed in 1946, aimed to resolve the political deadlock in India. Its key features included:

  1. The creation of a Union of India, comprising both British India and the princely states, with a dominion status.
  2. The establishment of a Constituent Assembly, elected by the provincial assemblies, to draft a constitution for the Union.
  3. The formation of an interim government, composed of representatives from the major political parties, to function until the Constituent Assembly completed its work.
