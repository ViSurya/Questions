Show the following on the map of the world : (1) New York (2) Cap - Cahira railway (3) Indian Ocean

1. **New York:** Located in the northeastern United States, New York City is a global metropolis known for its iconic skyline, cultural diversity, and financial significance.

2. **Cape-Cairo Railway:** This ambitious railway project, envisioned during the colonial era, aimed to connect Cape Town in South Africa to Cairo in Egypt. However, only sections of the railway were completed, and it remains a symbol of the challenges of infrastructure development in Africa.

3. **Indian Ocean:** The Indian Ocean, the third-largest ocean in the world, is bounded by Asia, Africa, Australia, and Antarctica. It is known for its diverse marine life, including coral reefs, and is a vital trade route connecting countries across the globe.
