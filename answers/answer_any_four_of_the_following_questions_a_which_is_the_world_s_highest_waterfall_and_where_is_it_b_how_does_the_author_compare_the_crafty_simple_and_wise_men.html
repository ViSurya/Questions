Answer any four of the following questions : (a) Which is the world s highest waterfall and where is it ? (b) How does the author compare the crafty. Simple and wise men

(a) The world's highest waterfall is Angel Falls, located in Venezuela. It has a height of 979 meters (3,212 feet) and is named after Jimmie Angel, an American aviator who first flew over it in 1933.

(b) The author compares the crafty, simple, and wise men by highlighting their distinct characteristics. The crafty man is cunning and deceitful, using cleverness to achieve his goals. The simple man is straightforward and honest, lacking complexity or guile. The wise man possesses knowledge, experience, and judgment, using his wisdom to make sound decisions.
