Express your opinion on Interviews

Interviews are a crucial part of the job application process, allowing employers to assess a candidate's qualifications, skills, and suitability for a particular position. They provide an opportunity for both the interviewer and the interviewee to engage in a meaningful conversation, exchange information, and make informed decisions. Interviews can be conducted in various formats, including face-to-face, telephone, video conferencing, or online assessments, and can range from structured to unstructured, depending on the organization's needs and preferences.
