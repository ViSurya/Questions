Write the components of Current Account.

The components of the Current Account are:

  1. Exports of goods and services: This includes all goods and services sold to other countries.
  2. Imports of goods and services: This includes all goods and services purchased from other countries.
  3. Net factor income from abroad: This includes interest, dividends, and profits earned by domestic residents from investments abroad, minus similar payments made to foreign residents.
  4. Net current transfers: This includes unilateral transfers, such as foreign aid, remittances, and gifts, received from abroad, minus similar payments made to foreign residents.
