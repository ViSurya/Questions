What is the significance of the title Journey to the End of the Earth?

The title "Journey to the End of the Earth" signifies a quest for the unknown, a voyage to the limits of human knowledge and experience. It suggests a journey that is both physical and metaphorical, a journey of discovery and self-discovery. The title evokes a sense of adventure, exploration, and the desire to push boundaries. It captures the essence of the human spirit's relentless pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
