If the position vectors of the points A and B are 7i ̂+3j ̂+k ̂ and 2i ̂+5j ̂+4k ̂ respectively, then find the magnitude of AB.

To find the magnitude of AB, we can use the distance formula between two points in three-dimensional space. The magnitude of AB is given by the formula:

$$|AB| = \sqrt{(x_B - x_A)^2 + (y_B - y_A)^2 + (z_B - z_A)^2}$$

Substituting the coordinates of points A and B, we get:

$$|AB| = \sqrt{(2 - 7)^2 + (5 - 3)^2 + (4 - 1)^2}$$

Simplifying the expression, we get:

$$|AB| = \sqrt{(-5)^2 + (2)^2 + (3)^2}$$

Further simplifying, we get:

$$|AB| = \sqrt{25 + 4 + 9}$$

Finally, we get:

$$|AB| = \sqrt{38}$$

Therefore, the magnitude of AB is √38.
