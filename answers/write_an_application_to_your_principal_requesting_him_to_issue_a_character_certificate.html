Write an application to your principal requesting him to issue a character certificate.

To,
The Principal, [School/College Name], [Address]

Date: [Date]

Subject: Request for Character Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam,

I, [Your Name], a student of Class 12, humbly request you to issue me a character certificate. I require this certificate for [Purpose of the Certificate]. I have been a student of this institution for the past [Number] years and have always strived to uphold the values and ethics of the school. I have consistently maintained a good academic record and have actively participated in various extracurricular activities.

I believe that a character certificate from you would be a valuable addition to my application and would greatly enhance my chances of success. I assure you that the certificate will be used solely for the purpose mentioned above.

I kindly request you to consider my request and issue me the character certificate at your earliest convenience. Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Yours sincerely,

[Your Name]
