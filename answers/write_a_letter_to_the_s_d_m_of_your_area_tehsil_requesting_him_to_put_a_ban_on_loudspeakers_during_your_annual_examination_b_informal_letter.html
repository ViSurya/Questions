Subject: Request for Ban on Loudspeakers During Annual Examination

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to bring to your attention a matter of great concern that is affecting the academic progress of students in our area during the annual examination period. The excessive use of loudspeakers, particularly during religious and social gatherings, has become a significant source of noise pollution, creating a disruptive environment for students who are trying to concentrate on their studies.

I kindly request your immediate intervention in this matter by implementing a temporary ban on the use of loudspeakers during the examination period. This measure would greatly alleviate the noise pollution and provide a conducive environment for students to focus on their studies.

I believe that this action is essential to ensure the well-being and academic success of our students. I appreciate your prompt attention to this matter and look forward to a positive response.

Thank you for your consideration.
