How to calculate the amount payable to retired partner ?

To calculate the amount payable to a retired partner, you need to consider the following factors:

  1. The partner's capital balance at the time of retirement.
  2. The value of the firm's assets and liabilities at the time of retirement.
  3. The terms of the partnership agreement regarding the payment of goodwill.

Once these factors have been considered, the amount payable to the retired partner can be calculated.
