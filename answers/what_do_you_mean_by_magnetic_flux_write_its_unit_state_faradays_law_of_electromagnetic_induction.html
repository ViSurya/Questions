What do you mean by magnetic flux ? Write its unit. State Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction.

Magnetic flux is the measure of the amount of magnetic field passing through a given area. Its unit is Weber (Wb) or Tesla-meter (T m). Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction states that the electromotive force (emf) induced in a conductor is equal to the negative of the rate of change of magnetic flux through the conductor.
