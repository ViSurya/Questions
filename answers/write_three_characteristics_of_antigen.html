Write three characteristics of Antigen.

Antigens are molecules that are recognized by the immune system as foreign and potentially harmful. They have three main characteristics:

  1. Immunogenicity: Antigens have the ability to stimulate an immune response, leading to the production of antibodies or activation of immune cells.
  2. Specificity: Each antigen has a unique structure that allows it to be recognized by specific antibodies or immune cells.
  3. Foreignness: Antigens are typically molecules that are not normally found in the body's own tissues.
