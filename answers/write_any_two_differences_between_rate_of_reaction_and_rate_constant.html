Write any two differences between Rate of reaction and Rate constant.

Rate of reaction and rate constant are two important concepts in chemical kinetics. The rate of reaction is the change in concentration of reactants or products per unit time, while the rate constant is a proportionality constant that relates the rate of reaction to the concentrations of the reactants.

Definition Change in concentration of reactants or products per unit time Proportionality constant that relates the rate of reaction to the concentrations of the reactants
