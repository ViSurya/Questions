Write any four causes for Cotton Industry developed in Mumbai.

The development of the cotton industry in Mumbai can be attributed to several factors:

  1. Mumbai's strategic location as a port city facilitated the import of raw cotton and the export of finished goods.
  2. The availability of cheap labor and a large domestic market for cotton textiles contributed to the industry's growth.
  3. The establishment of cotton mills in Mumbai during the British colonial period provided the necessary infrastructure for the industry's development.
  4. Government policies, such as the imposition of tariffs on imported textiles, provided protection to the domestic cotton industry.
