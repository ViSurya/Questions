What is self induction ? Write the unit of self inductance. Derive an expression for energy stored in current carrying coil.

Self-induction is the phenomenon in which an electromotive force (EMF) is induced in a conductor due to the change in its own magnetic field. The unit of self-inductance is Henry (H). The energy stored in a current-carrying coil is given by the formula: $$E = \frac{1}{2}LI^2$$ where E is the energy stored in joules (J), L is the self-inductance in henries (H), and I is the current in amperes (A).
