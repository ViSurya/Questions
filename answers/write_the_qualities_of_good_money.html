Write the qualities of good money.

Good money should possess several qualities to function effectively as a medium of exchange, store of value, and unit of account. These qualities include:

  1. Durability: Money should be able to withstand wear and tear during circulation and storage.
  2. Portability: Money should be easy to carry and transport, facilitating transactions.
  3. Divisibility: Money should be divisible into smaller units without losing its value, allowing for precise payments.
  4. Uniformity: Money should have a consistent quality and appearance, ensuring its acceptance and preventing counterfeiting.
  5. Stability: The value of money should remain relatively stable over time, preserving its purchasing power and facilitating long-term planning.
