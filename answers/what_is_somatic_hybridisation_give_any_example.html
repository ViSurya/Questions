What is somatic hybridisation ? Give any example.

Somatic hybridization is a technique that involves the fusion of somatic cells from two different species to create a hybrid cell. This process allows for the combination of genetic material from both species, resulting in a cell with a unique set of chromosomes. An example of somatic hybridization is the fusion of a human cell with a mouse cell to create a human-mouse hybrid cell. This hybrid cell would contain both human and mouse chromosomes, allowing researchers to study the interactions between the two species' genes.
