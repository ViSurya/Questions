Answer the question in about 60–80 words : How can one reach Chitrakoot ? Mention all the three ways.

To reach Chitrakoot, one can take three different routes: by road, by rail, or by air. The nearest airport is Khajuraho, which is about 175 kilometers away. The nearest railway station is Chitrakoot Dham Karwi, which is about 12 kilometers away. By road, one can take the National Highway 75 or the State Highway 52.
