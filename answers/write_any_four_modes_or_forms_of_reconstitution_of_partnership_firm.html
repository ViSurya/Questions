Write any four modes or forms of reconstitution of partnership firm.

There are four primary modes or forms of reconstitution of a partnership firm:

  1. Admission of a New Partner: A new partner is brought into the firm, resulting in a change in the profit-sharing ratio and capital structure.
  2. Retirement or Death of a Partner: A partner leaves the firm due to retirement, death, or expulsion, leading to a change in the composition of the partnership.
  3. Change in Profit-Sharing Ratio: The profit-sharing ratio among the partners is revised, altering the distribution of profits and losses.
  4. Introduction of New Capital: One or more partners contribute additional capital to the firm, modifying the capital structure and potentially the profit-sharing ratio.
