What is Dynamo ? Draw a labelled diagram and describe the working of an AC dynamo.

A dynamo is an electrical generator that converts mechanical energy to electrical energy. It consists of a rotating armature winding and a stationary field winding. The armature winding is connected to an external circuit, and the field winding is connected to a DC power source.

Figure: Diagram of an AC dynamo

When the armature winding rotates in the magnetic field produced by the field winding, an electromotive force (EMF) is induced in the armature winding. The EMF causes an alternating current (AC) to flow in the external circuit. The frequency of the AC is determined by the speed of rotation of the armature winding.
