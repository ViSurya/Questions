Define Test-tube baby.

A test-tube baby, also known as in vitro fertilization (IVF), is a process where fertilization takes place outside the body in a laboratory dish. The fertilized egg is then transferred to the woman's uterus, where it implants and develops like a naturally conceived baby. This technique is used to help couples who are unable to conceive naturally due to various factors such as infertility, blocked fallopian tubes, or other medical conditions.
