Current ratio is 3.5:1. Working capital is Rs. 90,000. Calculate the amount of current assets and current liabilities.

Given that the current ratio is 3.5:1 and the working capital is Rs. 90,000, we can calculate the amount of current assets and current liabilities as follows:

Current Assets = Current Ratio * Working Capital = 3.5 * 90,000 = Rs. 3,15,000
Current Liabilities = Working Capital / (Current Ratio + 1) = 90,000 / (3.5 + 1) = Rs. 20,000
