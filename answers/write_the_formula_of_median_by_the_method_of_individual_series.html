Write the formula of median by the method of individual series.

The formula for calculating the median by the method of individual series is:

Median = L + [(N/2 - F)/f] x C

Where:

  1. L = Lower limit of the median class
  2. N = Total number of observations
  3. F = Cumulative frequency of the class preceding the median class
  4. f = Frequency of the median class
  5. C = Class size
