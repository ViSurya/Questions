What was unusual about M Hamels dress on his last day in the school?

On his last day at school, Monsieur Hamel wore an unusual attire that caught the attention of his students. Instead of his customary everyday clothes, he donned his finest attire, a long black frock coat, a frilled shirt, and a colorful sash. This formal attire symbolized the significance of the occasion and conveyed his deep respect for the French language and culture, which he had dedicated his life to teaching.
