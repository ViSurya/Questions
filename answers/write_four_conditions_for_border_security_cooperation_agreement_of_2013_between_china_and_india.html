Write four conditions for border security cooperation agreement of 2013 between China and India.

The 2013 Border Security Cooperation Agreement between China and India was established on four primary conditions:

  1. Both nations agreed to respect each other's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
  2. They committed to maintaining peace and tranquility along their shared border.
  3. They established mechanisms for communication and consultation to address border-related issues.
  4. They agreed to cooperate in combating cross-border crimes, including terrorism, drug trafficking, and illegal migration.
