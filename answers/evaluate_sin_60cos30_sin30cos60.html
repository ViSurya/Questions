Evaluate : . sin 60°cos30° + sin30°cos60°

Using the trigonometric identities sin(A + B) = sinAcosB + cosAsinB and sin(A - B) = sinAcosB - cosAsinB, we can simplify the expression as follows:

Expression Simplification
sin 60°cos30° + sin30°cos60° (sin60°cos30° + cos60°sin30°) + (sin30°cos60° - cos60°sin30°)
= sin(60° + 30°) + sin(60° - 30°)
= sin90° + sin30°
= 1 + 1/2
= 3/2
