Delhi Police Advisory: Stay Vigilant Against Unclaimed Articles in Public Spaces

To ensure public safety, the Delhi Police urges citizens to remain cautious of unclaimed items such as dolls, tiffin boxes, suitcases, bags, transistors, and mobiles found in public places, trains, or buses. These articles may pose a security risk and should be reported immediately to the authorities.
