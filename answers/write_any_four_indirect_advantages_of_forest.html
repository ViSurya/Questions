Write any four indirect advantages of forest.

Forests offer a range of indirect advantages, including:

  1. Climate Regulation: Forests help regulate the climate by absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen, contributing to a balanced atmosphere.
  2. Water Cycle: Forests play a crucial role in the water cycle, absorbing and storing rainwater, releasing it slowly, and preventing floods and droughts.
  3. Biodiversity: Forests are home to a wide variety of plant and animal species, contributing to biodiversity and maintaining ecological balance.
  4. Soil Conservation: Forests help prevent soil erosion by holding the soil in place with their roots, reducing the risk of landslides and maintaining soil fertility.
