Write the effecting factors of commodity of supply.

The factors affecting the supply of a commodity are:

  1. Price of the commodity: As the price of a commodity increases, the quantity supplied also increases, and vice versa.
  2. Cost of production: If the cost of producing a commodity increases, the quantity supplied decreases, and vice versa.
  3. Technology: Improvements in technology can reduce the cost of production, leading to an increase in the quantity supplied.
  4. Government policies: Government policies such as subsidies, taxes, and regulations can affect the quantity supplied.
  5. Natural factors: Natural factors such as weather conditions and natural disasters can affect the quantity supplied of agricultural commodities.
