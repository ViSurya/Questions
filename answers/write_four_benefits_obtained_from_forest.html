Write four benefits obtained from forest.

Forests provide numerous benefits, including:

  1. Oxygen Production: Forests absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen through photosynthesis, contributing to the Earth's oxygen supply.
  2. Water Cycle Regulation: Forests help regulate the water cycle by absorbing rainwater, reducing runoff, and releasing water vapor into the atmosphere.
  3. Biodiversity Conservation: Forests are home to a wide variety of plant and animal species, contributing to biodiversity and ecological balance.
  4. Economic Benefits: Forests provide timber, fuelwood, and other forest products, supporting livelihoods and contributing to economic development.
