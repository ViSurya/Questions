Write any two characteristics of Equity Shares.

Equity shares are a type of security that represents ownership in a company. They carry certain characteristics that distinguish them from other types of shares.

  1. Voting Rights: Equity shareholders have the right to vote in shareholder meetings, which gives them a say in the company's management and decision-making.
  2. Residual Claim on Assets: In the event of a company's liquidation, equity shareholders have a residual claim on the company's assets after all other creditors and stakeholders have been paid.
