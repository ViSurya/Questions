Show that the lines x + 3 / –3 = y – 1 / 1 = z – 5 / 5 and x + 1 / - 1 = y – 2 / 2 = z – 5 / 5 are coplanar.

To show that the given lines are coplanar, we can determine if they intersect at a common point. If they do, then they lie on the same plane.

Solving for the point of intersection, we find that both lines pass through the point (1, 3, 5). Therefore, the lines are coplanar.
