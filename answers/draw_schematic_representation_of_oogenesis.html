Draw schematic representation of Oogenesis.

Oogenesis is the process of formation of a mature female gamete, or ovum, from an oogonium. It involves a series of cell divisions and cellular changes.

  1. It begins with the oogonium, which undergoes mitosis to produce primary oocytes.
  2. The primary oocytes then undergo the first meiotic division to form secondary oocytes and polar bodies.
  3. The secondary oocytes undergo the second meiotic division to form an ovum and another polar body.
