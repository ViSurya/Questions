Evaluate ∫〖((x^2+1) e^x)/(x+1)^2 dx〗

To evaluate the integral ∫〖((x^2+1) e^x)/(x+1)^2 dx〗, we can use integration by parts. Let u = x^2 + 1 and dv = e^x/(x+1)^2 dx. Then du = 2x dx and v = -1/(x+1). Substituting these into the integral, we get:

∫〖((x^2+1) e^x)/(x+1)^2 dx〗 = -1/(x+1) * (x^2 + 1) + ∫1/(x+1) * 2x dx
= -1/(x+1) * (x^2 + 1) + 2∫1/(x+1) * x dx = -1/(x+1) * (x^2 + 1) + 2ln|x+1| + C

where C is the constant of integration.
