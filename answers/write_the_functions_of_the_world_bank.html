Write the functions of the World Bank.

The World Bank, a vital international financial institution, performs several crucial functions:

  1. It provides financial assistance to developing countries for various projects, including infrastructure development, education, and healthcare.
  2. The World Bank offers technical assistance and policy advice to help countries improve their economic and social policies.
  3. It promotes international trade and investment by providing loans and guarantees to businesses and governments.
  4. The World Bank works to reduce poverty and inequality by supporting programs that aim to improve the living standards of the poor and vulnerable.
