Formal Letters : Write an application to the Principal requesting him to issue you a character certificate as you

To,
The Principal,
[School/College Name],
[Address]

Date: [Date]

Subject: Request for Character Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to request a character certificate from your esteemed institution. I am a student of [Class] in your school and have been a part of this institution for the past [Number] years. I have always strived to uphold the values and ethics of the school and have actively participated in various extracurricular activities.

I require this certificate for [Purpose of Certificate]. I assure you that the certificate will be used solely for the intended purpose and will not be misused in any way.

I kindly request you to issue me the character certificate at your earliest convenience. I would be grateful if you could provide me with the certificate by [Date].

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Yours sincerely,

[Your Name]

[Class]

[Roll Number]
