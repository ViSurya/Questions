Write any four characteristics of scientific management.

Scientific management, developed by Frederick Winslow Taylor, emphasizes the use of scientific methods to improve organizational efficiency and productivity. Its key characteristics include:

  1. Division of Labor: Tasks are divided into smaller, specialized steps, allowing workers to become experts in their specific tasks.
  2. Time and Motion Study: Scientific analysis of work processes to identify and eliminate unnecessary steps, leading to increased efficiency.
  3. Standardization: Establishing uniform procedures, tools, and methods to ensure consistency and quality in production.
  4. Scientific Selection and Training: Workers are selected based on their abilities and provided proper training to enhance their skills and productivity.
