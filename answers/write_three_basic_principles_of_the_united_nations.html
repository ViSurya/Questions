Write three basic principles of the United Nations.

The United Nations is guided by three fundamental principles:

  1. Maintaining international peace and security: The UN works to prevent conflicts, promote peaceful resolutions, and take collective action to address threats to peace.
  2. Promoting human rights: The UN advocates for the protection and promotion of human rights for all individuals, regardless of race, gender, religion, or nationality.
  3. Fostering international cooperation: The UN facilitates cooperation among nations in addressing global challenges such as climate change, poverty, and disease, and promotes economic and social development.
