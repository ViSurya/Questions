Informal letters : Write a letter to your friend Sujata inviting her to spend the summer vacation at your place B

Dearest Sujata, I hope this letter finds you in high spirits. I'm writing to extend an invitation for you to spend the upcoming summer vacation at my place in [Your City]. It would be wonderful to have you here and share some unforgettable moments together.

We have planned various exciting activities to make the most of the summer. We can go for refreshing swims in the nearby lake, explore the scenic hiking trails, and visit the amusement park for some thrilling rides. Additionally, we can indulge in movie marathons, play board games, and have cozy campfires under the starry sky.

I know you've been looking forward to a relaxing and adventurous vacation, and I believe spending it here would be the perfect blend of both. Your presence would make this summer truly memorable. Please let me know if you can make it. I can't wait to host you and create lasting memories together.

Warmest regards, [Your Name]
