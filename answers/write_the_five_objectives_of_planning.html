Write the five objectives of planning.

Planning in India aims to achieve specific objectives that contribute to the overall development and well-being of the nation. These objectives include:

  1. Rapid Economic Growth: Planning seeks to accelerate the pace of economic growth by promoting industrialization, agriculture, and infrastructure development.
  2. Social Justice and Equity: Planning aims to reduce economic and social disparities by providing equal opportunities, access to education, healthcare, and employment.
  3. Self-Reliance: Planning emphasizes the importance of self-sufficiency in critical sectors, reducing dependence on imports and promoting domestic production.
  4. Modernization: Planning aims to modernize the economy, infrastructure, and technology to enhance productivity and competitiveness.
  5. Sustainable Development: Planning considers the long-term impact of economic activities on the environment and promotes sustainable practices to ensure intergenerational equity.
