If G is the centroid of any triangle then show that (prove that) GA ⃗+GB ⃗+GC ⃗=0 ⃗

Let A, B, and C be the vertices of a triangle and G be its centroid. Then, we can prove that \(\overrightarrow{GA} + \overrightarrow{GB} + \overrightarrow{GC} = \overrightarrow{0}\).

Since G is the centroid, we know that \(\overrightarrow{GA} + \overrightarrow{GB} + \overrightarrow{GC} = 3\overrightarrow{GG} = \overrightarrow{0}\).
