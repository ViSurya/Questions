Write any two characteristics of a Company.

Characteristics of a Company:

  1. Separate Legal Entity: A company is a separate legal entity from its owners, known as shareholders. It can enter into contracts, own property, and sue or be sued in its own name.
  2. Limited Liability: Shareholders' liability is limited to the extent of their investment in the company. This means that they are not personally liable for the debts and obligations of the company.
