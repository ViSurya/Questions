Formal Letters : Write an application to the principal of your school requesting him to issue a School Leaving Ce

To The Principal, [School Name], [School Address],

Subject: Request for School Leaving Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam, I, [Your Name], a student of Class 12, humbly request you to kindly issue me a School Leaving Certificate. I have completed my studies at your esteemed institution and am now seeking higher education opportunities.

I assure you that the certificate will be utilized solely for educational purposes and will not be misused in any way. I kindly request you to process my application promptly, as I need the certificate for admission to my chosen college.

Thanking you in anticipation of your kind consideration.

Yours sincerely, [Your Name] [Class and Section] [Roll Number]
