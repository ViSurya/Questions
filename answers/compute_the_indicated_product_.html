Compute the indicated product:

(x + 2)(x2 - 2x + 4)

To compute the product, we can use the distributive property:

(x + 2)(x2 - 2x + 4) = x(x2 - 2x + 4) + 2(x2 - 2x + 4)

Expanding each term, we get:

x3 - 2x2 + 4x + 2x2 - 4x + 8

Combining like terms, we get:

x3 + 4 + 8

Therefore, the product of (x + 2)(x2 - 2x + 4) is x3 + 4 + 8.
