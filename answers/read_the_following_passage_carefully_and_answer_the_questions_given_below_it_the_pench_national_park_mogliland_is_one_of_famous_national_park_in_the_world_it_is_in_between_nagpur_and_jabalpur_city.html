The Pench National Park, also known as Mogliland, is a renowned national park located between the cities of Nagpur and Jabalpur in India. It is famous for its diverse wildlife, including tigers, leopards, wild dogs, and a variety of bird species. The park offers visitors an opportunity to experience the beauty of nature and witness the rich biodiversity of the region.
