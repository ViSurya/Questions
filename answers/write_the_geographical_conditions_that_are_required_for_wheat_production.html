Write the geographical conditions that are required for wheat production.

Wheat cultivation requires specific geographical conditions to thrive. These conditions include:

  1. Temperature: Wheat prefers a cool growing season with average temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C.
  2. Rainfall: Wheat requires moderate rainfall, typically between 500mm and 750mm, evenly distributed throughout the growing season.
  3. Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil with a pH between 6.0 and 7.5 is ideal for wheat cultivation.
  4. Sunlight: Wheat requires ample sunlight, at least 6 hours per day, for proper growth and development.
