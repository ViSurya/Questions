What is the suggested reasons for population explosion? (any two)

The rapid increase in population, known as population explosion, can be attributed to several factors. Two significant reasons include:

  1. Improved Healthcare: Advancements in medical science and public health measures have led to a decline in mortality rates, resulting in increased life expectancy and a larger population.
  2. Socioeconomic Factors: Factors such as improved living conditions, access to education, and economic stability have contributed to a decrease in fertility rates, leading to a population increase.
