Write sources and deficiency diseases from vitamin A, C and D.

Vitamin A: Sources include carrots, spinach, and liver. Deficiency can lead to night blindness and dry eyes.

Vitamin C: Sources include oranges, lemons, and tomatoes. Deficiency can cause scurvy, characterized by bleeding gums and impaired wound healing.

Vitamin D: Sources include sunlight, fortified milk, and fatty fish. Deficiency can result in rickets, causing bone deformities, and osteomalacia, leading to bone pain and weakness.
