What do you mean by Star Topology ? Write its advantages and disadvantages.

In a Star Topology, all devices are connected to a central node, typically a switch or a hub. This central node acts as a controller and manages the flow of data between the devices.

Advantages:

  1. Easy to install and maintain.
  2. Scalable, allowing for easy addition or removal of devices.
  3. Centralized control, making it easier to manage and troubleshoot the network.
Disadvantages:
  1. A single point of failure, as the failure of the central node can disrupt the entire network.
  2. More expensive than other topologies due to the need for a central node and additional cabling.
