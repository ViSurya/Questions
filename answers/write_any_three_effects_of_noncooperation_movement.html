Write any three effects of Non-Cooperation Movement.

The Non-Cooperation Movement had several significant effects:

  1. It led to the boycott of British goods, educational institutions, and law courts, resulting in a decline in British trade and influence in India.
  2. The movement fostered a sense of unity and nationalism among Indians, strengthening the struggle for independence.
  3. It brought Mahatma Gandhi to the forefront of the Indian independence movement, establishing him as a prominent leader.
