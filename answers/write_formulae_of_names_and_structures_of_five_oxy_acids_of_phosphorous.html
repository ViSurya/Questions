Write formulae of names and structures of five oxy acids of phosphorous.

The five oxy acids of phosphorus are:

  1. Hypophosphorous acid (H3PO2): It is a diprotic acid with the structure H-P(O)H2.
  2. Phosphorous acid (H3PO3): It is a triprotic acid with the structure H-P(O)(OH)2.
  3. Phosphoric acid (H3PO4): It is a triprotic acid with the structure H3PO4.
  4. Pyrophosphoric acid (H4P2O7): It is a tetrabasic acid with the structure H4P2O7.
  5. Metaphosphoric acid (HPO3): It is a monobasic acid with the structure HPO3.
