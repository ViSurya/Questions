What are the objectives of government budget ? Write.

The government budget aims to achieve several objectives, including:

  1. Resource Allocation: Efficiently allocating resources among various sectors of the economy to promote economic growth and development.
  2. Income Redistribution: Reducing income inequality by transferring resources from the wealthy to the poor through progressive taxation and social welfare programs.
  3. Economic Stabilization: Managing aggregate demand to achieve price stability, full employment, and sustainable economic growth.
