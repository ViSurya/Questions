Find AB, if A=[(0 -1,0 2)] and B=[(3 5,0 0)].

To find the distance AB, we can use the distance formula between two points in a plane: AB = √[(xB - xA)2 + (yB - yA)2].

Plugging in the coordinates of points A and B, we get: AB = √[(3 - 0)2 + (5 - (-1))2] = √[9 + 36] = √45 = 3√5.

Therefore, the distance between points A and B is 3√5 units.
