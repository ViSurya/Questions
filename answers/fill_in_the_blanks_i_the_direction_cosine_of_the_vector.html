Fill in the blanks. (i) The direction cosine of the vector

The direction cosine of the vector \( \overrightarrow{a} = a_1 \hat{i} + a_2 \hat{j} + a_3 \hat{k} \) are \( \cos \alpha = \frac{a_1}{\sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2}} \), \( \cos \beta = \frac{a_2}{\sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2}} \), and \( \cos \gamma = \frac{a_3}{\sqrt{a_1^2 + a_2^2 + a_3^2}} \).
