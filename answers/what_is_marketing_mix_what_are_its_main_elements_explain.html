What is Marketing Mix ? What are its main elements ? Explain.

Marketing mix refers to the combination of four key elements that a company uses to market its products or services to its target audience. These elements are product, price, place, and promotion.

  1. Product: This includes the physical attributes, features, and benefits of the product or service.
  2. Price: This refers to the amount of money that customers are willing to pay for the product or service.
  3. Place: This involves the channels of distribution used to make the product or service available to customers.
  4. Promotion: This includes the various methods used to communicate the product or service to potential customers and persuade them to make a purchase.
