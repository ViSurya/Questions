In the face of insecurity, fear, war, worry, and stress that pervade the modern world, we employ various coping mechanisms to overcome these challenges. We seek solace in religion, spirituality, and meditation to find inner peace and resilience. We engage in self-care practices like exercise, healthy eating, and relaxation techniques to maintain physical and mental well-being. We also turn to social support networks, such as family, friends, and community groups, for emotional comfort and guidance. Additionally, we actively participate in social and political movements to address the root causes of these issues and work towards creating a more just and equitable society.
