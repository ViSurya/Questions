Show the following on the map of India : (i) Thar desert (ii) Cotton productive area (iii) Plateau of Chhota Nagpur

The Thar Desert, a vast expanse of arid land, is located in the western part of India, covering parts of Rajasthan and Gujarat. The cotton-producing areas are primarily concentrated in the black soil regions of Maharashtra, Gujarat, and parts of Madhya Pradesh. The Plateau of Chhota Nagpur is situated in the eastern part of India, encompassing parts of Jharkhand, Odisha, and Chhattisgarh.
