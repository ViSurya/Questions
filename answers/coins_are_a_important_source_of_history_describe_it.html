Coins are a important source of History ? Describe it.

Coins are a valuable source of historical information, providing insights into the economic, political, and cultural aspects of past civilizations. They often bear images of rulers, deities, or symbols, inscriptions in various languages, and dates or mint marks that help historians understand the chronology and geography of ancient kingdoms. Additionally, the composition and weight of coins can reveal information about the monetary systems and trade practices of the time. By studying coins, historians can gain a deeper understanding of the economic and political structures, artistic styles, and religious beliefs of past societies.
