Write an application to the principal of your school requesting him to issue a School Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate.

To,
The Principal,
[School Name],
[School Address]

Date: [Date]

Subject: Request for School Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam,

I, [Your Name], a student of class [Your Class] in your esteemed school, humbly request you to kindly issue me a School Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate. I am leaving the school due to [Reason for Leaving].

I have been a student of this school for [Number of Years] years and have always strived to uphold the values and standards set by the institution. I am grateful for the knowledge and guidance I have received from my teachers and the opportunities provided by the school to develop my academic, extracurricular, and social skills.

I request you to kindly process my application and issue the necessary certificate at the earliest convenience. I assure you that all dues and obligations towards the school have been cleared.

Thanking you for your kind consideration.

Yours sincerely,

[Your Name]
