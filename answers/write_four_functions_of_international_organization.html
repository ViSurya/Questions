Write four functions of International Organization,

International organizations play a crucial role in the global arena, performing various functions that contribute to international cooperation and stability. These functions include:

  1. Promoting Peace and Security: International organizations work to prevent and resolve conflicts, promote disarmament, and maintain international peace and security.
  2. Fostering Economic Cooperation: They facilitate international trade, promote economic development, and provide financial assistance to developing countries.
  3. Protecting Human Rights: International organizations monitor and promote human rights, advocating for the protection of individuals and groups from discrimination and abuse.
  4. Addressing Global Challenges: They address transnational issues such as climate change, environmental degradation, and public health emergencies, requiring collective action.
