Note Making : In the world have we made health an end in itself? We have forgotten that health is really a means

```html

In our pursuit of health, we have mistakenly elevated it to an end in itself, losing sight of its true purpose as a means to a fulfilling life. This misguided focus has led us to prioritize health above all else, often at the expense of other important aspects of human existence, such as personal growth, relationships, and meaningful experiences.
```
