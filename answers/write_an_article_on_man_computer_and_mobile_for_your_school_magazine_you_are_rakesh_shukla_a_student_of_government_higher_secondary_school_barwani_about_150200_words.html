Write an article on Man, Computer and Mobile for your school magazine. You are Rakesh Shukla, a student of Government Higher Secondary School, Barwani. (about 150-200 words).

In the modern era, the relationship between humans, computers, and mobile devices has become increasingly intertwined. Computers have revolutionized the way we process information, communicate, and access knowledge, while mobile devices have made it possible to stay connected and productive on the go.

The convergence of these technologies has led to a new era of digital transformation, where the boundaries between the physical and virtual worlds are becoming increasingly blurred.
