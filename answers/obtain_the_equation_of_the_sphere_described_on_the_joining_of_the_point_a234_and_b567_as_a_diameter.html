Obtain the equation of the sphere described on the joining of the point A(2,-3,4) and B(-5,6,7) as a diameter.

The equation of the sphere with diameter AB is given by: $$(x - \frac{2-5}{2})^2 + (y + \frac{3+6}{2})^2 + (z - \frac{4+7}{2})^2 = \left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{9}{2}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{11}{2}\right)^2$$

Simplifying the equation, we get: $$(x - \frac{7}{2})^2 + (y + \frac{9}{2})^2 + (z - \frac{11}{2})^2 = \frac{361}{4}$$
