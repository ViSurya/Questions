Write the types of Preference Shares.

Preference shares are classified into various types based on their features and characteristics. Some common types of preference shares include:

  1. Cumulative Preference Shares: These shares carry the right to receive dividends in arrears if they are not paid in a particular year.
  2. Non-Cumulative Preference Shares: These shares do not carry the right to receive dividends in arrears if they are not paid in a particular year.
  3. Participating Preference Shares: These shares carry the right to participate in the surplus profits of the company after the equity shareholders have received a specified dividend.
  4. Non-Participating Preference Shares: These shares do not carry the right to participate in the surplus profits of the company.
  5. Redeemable Preference Shares: These shares can be redeemed by the company at a specified future date or under certain conditions.
  6. Irredeemable Preference Shares: These shares cannot be redeemed by the company.
