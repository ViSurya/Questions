Write meaning of co-operation and write five reasons of its failure.

Cooperation is the process of working together to achieve a common goal. It involves mutual trust, understanding, and a willingness to compromise. Cooperation can be between individuals, groups, or even countries.

There are several reasons why cooperation may fail. These include:

  1. Lack of trust: If parties do not trust each other, they are less likely to cooperate.
  2. Conflicting interests: If parties have different goals, they may be unable to find a mutually acceptable solution.
  3. Lack of communication: If parties are unable to communicate effectively, they may misunderstand each other's intentions.
  4. Power imbalances: If one party has more power than the other, the weaker party may be reluctant to cooperate.
  5. Free riding: If one party can benefit from the cooperation without contributing, they may be tempted to free ride.
