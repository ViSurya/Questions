What do you understand by the environment ? Write five types of environmental pollution,

The environment encompasses all the living and non-living things that surround us, including the air we breathe, the water we drink, the land we live on, and the plants and animals that inhabit it. Environmental pollution refers to the contamination of these natural resources, leading to harmful effects on human health and the overall ecosystem.

  1. Air Pollution: Emission of harmful gases, particulate matter, and toxic chemicals into the atmosphere, primarily caused by industrial activities, vehicular emissions, and deforestation.
  2. Water Pollution: Contamination of water bodies, such as rivers, lakes, and oceans, with pollutants like sewage, industrial waste, agricultural runoff, and oil spills.
  3. Land Pollution: Accumulation of solid waste, hazardous chemicals, and non-biodegradable materials on land, resulting from improper waste disposal, mining activities, and deforestation.
  4. Noise Pollution: Excessive and unwanted sounds that can cause hearing impairment, stress, and sleep disturbances, primarily caused by industrial machinery, construction activities, and transportation.
  5. Light Pollution: Excessive artificial light that disrupts natural light cycles, affecting wildlife, human health, and astronomical observations.
