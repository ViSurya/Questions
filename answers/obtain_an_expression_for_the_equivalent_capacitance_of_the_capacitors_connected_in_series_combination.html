Obtain an expression for the equivalent capacitance of the capacitors connected in series combination.

When capacitors are connected in series, the equivalent capacitance (Ceq) is given by the formula:

Equivalent Capacitance $$C_{eq} = \frac{1}{\frac{1}{C_1} + \frac{1}{C_2} + ... + \frac{1}{C_n}}$$

where C1, C2, ..., Cn are the capacitances of the individual capacitors.
