Write any five reasons for the development of the textiles industry in Mumbai.

The development of the textiles industry in Mumbai can be attributed to several factors:

  1. Mumbai's strategic location as a port city facilitated easy access to raw materials and export markets.
  2. The city's proximity to cotton-growing regions in Maharashtra and Gujarat ensured a steady supply of raw materials.
  3. The availability of cheap labor and skilled workers attracted textile manufacturers to Mumbai.
  4. The city's well-developed infrastructure, including transportation networks and banking facilities, supported the growth of the industry.
  5. Government policies, such as the imposition of tariffs on imported textiles, provided protection to the domestic industry.
