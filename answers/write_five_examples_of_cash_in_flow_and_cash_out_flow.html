Write five examples of cash in flow and cash out flow.

Cash Inflow:

  1. Sale of goods or services
  2. Receipt of loans or investments
  3. Interest or dividend income
  4. Rental income
  5. Government grants or subsidies
Cash Outflow:
  1. Purchase of goods or services
  2. Repayment of loans or investments
  3. Payment of salaries and wages
  4. Payment of rent or utilities
  5. Taxes
