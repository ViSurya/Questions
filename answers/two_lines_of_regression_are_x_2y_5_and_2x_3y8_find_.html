Two lines of regression are x +2y = 5 and 2x + 3y=8, find :

To find the angle between the two lines of regression, we can use the formula: $$\tan \theta = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$$ where \( (x_1, y_1) \) and \( (x_2, y_2) \) are points on the two lines, respectively.

Substituting the given values, we get: $$\tan \theta = \frac{3 - 2}{8 - 5} = \frac{1}{3}$$

Therefore, the angle between the two lines of regression is \( \theta = \arctan \left( \frac{1}{3} \right) \approx 18.43^\circ \).
