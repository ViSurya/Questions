Write any four reasons of destruction in Indus civilization.

The Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's earliest civilizations, met its demise due to a combination of factors. These include:

  1. Climate Change: Drastic changes in the course of the Indus River and the drying up of the Saraswati River led to a decline in agriculture and a shortage of water.
  2. Natural Disasters: Frequent floods and earthquakes in the region further weakened the civilization's infrastructure and resources.
  3. Overexploitation of Resources: The excessive use of natural resources, such as deforestation and the depletion of water sources, contributed to environmental degradation.
  4. Internal Conflicts and Invasions: Political instability, internal conflicts, and invasions by nomadic tribes from Central Asia further weakened the civilization's defenses and led to its eventual collapse.
