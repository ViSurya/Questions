If the radius of a sphere is measured as 9 cm with an error 0.03 cm, then find the approximate error in calculating its volume.

Let \(V\) be the volume of the sphere. Then, the approximate error in calculating the volume is given by $$dV = \frac{dV}{dr}dr = 4\pi r^2 dr$$ Substituting \(r = 9\) cm and \(dr = 0.03\) cm, we get $$dV = 4\pi (9)^2 (0.03) = 32.4\pi \approx 102.12 \text{ cm}^3$$
