Give five measures to eliminate the Illiteracy in India.

To eliminate illiteracy in India, several measures can be taken:

  1. Expand access to education: Ensure that all children have access to free and compulsory education up to the secondary level.

  2. Improve the quality of education: Focus on improving the quality of teaching and learning, providing adequate resources, and training teachers effectively.

  3. Promote adult literacy programs: Organize adult literacy programs to provide opportunities for those who missed out on formal education to acquire basic literacy skills.

  4. Utilize technology: Leverage technology to provide innovative and accessible learning platforms, such as online courses and mobile learning apps.

  5. Raise awareness and encourage community involvement: Conduct awareness campaigns to emphasize the importance of literacy and encourage communities to support and participate in literacy initiatives.
