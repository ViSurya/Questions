Write down several steps of recombinant DNA technology in sequence.

Recombinant DNA technology involves several steps:

  1. Isolation of the gene of interest from the donor organism.
  2. Insertion of the gene into a cloning vector, such as a plasmid or virus.
  3. Introduction of the recombinant DNA into a host organism, usually a bacterium.
  4. Selection and isolation of the host cells that have successfully taken up the recombinant DNA.
  5. Expression of the gene of interest in the host organism, leading to the production of the desired protein.
