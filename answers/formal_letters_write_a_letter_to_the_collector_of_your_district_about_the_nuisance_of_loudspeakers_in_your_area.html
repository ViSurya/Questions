Formal Letters : Write a letter to the collector of your district about the nuisance of loudspeakers in your area

In a letter addressed to the Collector of the district, I expressed my concern about the nuisance caused by loudspeakers in my area. I highlighted the excessive noise pollution, particularly during festivals and religious gatherings, which disrupts the peace and tranquility of the neighborhood. I emphasized the need for strict enforcement of noise control regulations and requested the Collector's intervention to address this issue and ensure a peaceful environment for all residents.
