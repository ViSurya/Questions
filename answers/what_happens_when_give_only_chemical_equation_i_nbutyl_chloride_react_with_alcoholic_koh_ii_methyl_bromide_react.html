What happens when : (Give only chemical equation) (i) n-Butyl chloride react with alcoholic KOH . (ii) Methyl bromide react

Chemical Equations:

  1. n-Butyl chloride reacts with alcoholic KOH to undergo substitution reaction, forming 1-butanol:
    C4H9Cl + KOH (alc.) → C4H9OH + KCl
  2. Methyl bromide reacts with water in a nucleophilic substitution reaction, forming methanol and hydrogen bromide:
    CH3Br + H2O → CH3OH + HBr
