Write any four reasons as to why India adopted the policy of Non-alignment.

India adopted the policy of Non-alignment due to several reasons:

  1. To maintain its independence and sovereignty in international relations.
  2. To avoid getting entangled in the Cold War rivalry between the United States and the Soviet Union.
  3. To promote peace and cooperation among nations.
  4. To support the rights of developing countries and to advocate for a more just and equitable international order.
