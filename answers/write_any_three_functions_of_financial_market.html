Write any three functions of financial market.

Financial markets perform three primary functions:

  1. Capital Formation: Financial markets facilitate the mobilization of savings from surplus units (individuals, households, and businesses) to deficit units (businesses, governments, and individuals seeking loans) for productive investment.
  2. Price Discovery: Financial markets determine the prices of financial assets (stocks, bonds, currencies, etc.) through the interaction of buyers and sellers. These prices reflect the market's assessment of the value of these assets.
  3. Risk Transfer: Financial markets allow for the transfer of risk from one party to another. For example, insurance companies take on the risk of loss or damage from individuals and businesses in exchange for a premium.
