A 5 cm long object is placed in front of a concave mirror of radius of curvature 40 cm. The distance of object from mirror is 40 cm. Find the distance and size of the image.

Using the mirror equation, $$\frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{f}$$ and the magnification equation, $$m = \frac{-v}{u}$$ we can find that the image is formed 20 cm behind the mirror and is 2.5 cm long.
