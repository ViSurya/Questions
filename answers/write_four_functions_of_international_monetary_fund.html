Write four functions of International Monetary Fund.

The International Monetary Fund (IMF) performs four primary functions:

  1. Lending Money: Provides financial assistance to member countries facing balance of payments problems.
  2. Promoting Exchange Rate Stability: Works to stabilize exchange rates and prevent disruptive currency fluctuations.
  3. Providing Policy Advice: Offers economic and financial policy advice to member countries to help them achieve sustainable economic growth.
  4. Technical Assistance: Provides technical assistance and training to member countries to help them strengthen their economic institutions and policies.
