Write any three objectives of Swachh Bharat Mission.

The Swachh Bharat Mission, also known as the Clean India Mission, aims to achieve a clean and open defecation-free India by 2019. Its objectives include:

  1. Eliminating open defecation and improving solid waste management.
  2. Eradicating manual scavenging and creating a clean and hygienic environment.
  3. Promoting behavioral change and generating awareness about sanitation and hygiene.
