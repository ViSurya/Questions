Describe the direct benefits of forests. (any three)

Forests provide a wide range of direct benefits, including:

  1. Timber and Non-Timber Forest Products: Forests provide timber for construction, furniture, and other wood products. They also provide non-timber forest products such as fruits, nuts, and medicinal plants.
  2. Fuelwood and Fodder: Forests provide fuelwood for cooking and heating, and fodder for livestock.
  3. Environmental Services: Forests help regulate the climate, protect watersheds, and provide habitat for wildlife.
