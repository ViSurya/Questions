Discuss the principles of direction. (any three)

The principles of direction are a set of guidelines that help managers effectively lead and motivate their teams. Three key principles include:

  1. Unity of Command: Each subordinate should receive orders and be accountable to only one superior.
  2. Scalar Chain: A clear line of authority should exist from top to bottom, ensuring that all levels of management are connected.
  3. Esprit de Corps: A sense of unity, loyalty, and teamwork should be fostered among employees to enhance organizational effectiveness.
