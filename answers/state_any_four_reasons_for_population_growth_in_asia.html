State any four reasons for population growth in Asia.

Asia's population growth can be attributed to several factors:

  1. High fertility rates, particularly in some countries, contribute to a larger population base.
  2. Improved healthcare and medical facilities have led to a decline in mortality rates, increasing life expectancy.
  3. Cultural and religious beliefs in some regions emphasize the importance of large families.
  4. Migration from rural to urban areas in search of better economic opportunities also contributes to population growth in urban centers.
