Summarize the poem “We are the Future in about 80 words.

The poem "We are the Future" expresses the aspirations and determination of the younger generation to shape a better world. The youth, as the future leaders, are portrayed as possessing the power to bring about positive change and progress. They are depicted as being equipped with knowledge, passion, and a sense of responsibility to address global issues and create a more just and sustainable society. The poem emphasizes the importance of unity, collaboration, and the collective efforts of the youth in working towards a brighter future for all.
