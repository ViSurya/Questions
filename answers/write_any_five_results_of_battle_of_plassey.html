Write any five results of battle of Plassey.

The Battle of Plassey, fought in 1757, had several significant results:

  1. The British East India Company gained control over Bengal, a wealthy and strategically important region in India.
  2. The victory of the British marked the beginning of their political and economic dominance in India.
  3. The defeat of the Nawab of Bengal, Siraj-ud-Daulah, weakened the Mughal Empire and accelerated its decline.
  4. The battle led to the rise of the British East India Company as a major political and military power in India.
  5. The victory of the British paved the way for the eventual British colonization of India.
