Write the procedure of Forfeiture of shares.

Forfeiture of shares is a process by which a company can cancel the shares of a shareholder who fails to pay the calls made on those shares. The procedure for forfeiting shares typically involves the following steps:

  1. The company sends a notice to the shareholder demanding payment of the unpaid calls.
  2. If the shareholder fails to pay the calls within the specified time, the company may resolve to sell the shares.
  3. The company then sells the shares at the best price obtainable.
  4. The proceeds of the sale are applied to pay the unpaid calls and any expenses incurred in the sale.
  5. Any surplus proceeds are paid to the shareholder.
