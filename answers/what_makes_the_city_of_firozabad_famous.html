What makes the city of Firozabad famous?

Firozabad is renowned for its flourishing glass industry, earning it the title of "Glass City of India." The city is home to numerous glass factories and workshops, producing a wide range of glass products, including bangles, beads, chandeliers, and decorative items. Firozabad's glass artisans possess exceptional skills and techniques, creating exquisite and intricate designs that have gained recognition both domestically and internationally.
