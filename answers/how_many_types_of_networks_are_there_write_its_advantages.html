How many types of Networks are there? Write its advantages.

There are two main types of networks: Local Area Networks (LANs) and Wide Area Networks (WANs). LANs connect devices within a limited physical area, such as a home or office, while WANs connect devices over a larger geographical area, such as a city or country.

LANs offer advantages such as high speed, low latency, and ease of management, while WANs provide connectivity over long distances and enable communication between geographically dispersed devices.
