Discuss non- financial incentives. (any three)

Non-financial incentives are rewards that do not involve monetary compensation. They can be used to motivate employees and improve their performance. Three common non-financial incentives include:

  1. Recognition and Appreciation: Publicly acknowledging and appreciating employees' contributions can boost their morale and sense of value within the organization.
  2. Opportunities for Growth and Development: Providing employees with chances for skill enhancement, training, and career advancement can increase their job satisfaction and loyalty.
  3. Flexible Work Arrangements: Offering flexible work hours, remote work options, or customized schedules can improve employees' work-life balance and overall well-being.
