Write the names of any two types of cyber crime.

Cybercrime encompasses a wide range of illegal activities conducted through computers and the internet. Two common types of cybercrime include:

  1. Phishing:

    Phishing involves sending fraudulent emails or messages designed to trick individuals into revealing personal information, such as passwords or credit card numbers.

  2. Malware Attacks:

    Malware attacks involve the distribution of malicious software, such as viruses, worms, or spyware, to gain unauthorized access to computer systems or steal sensitive data.
