Write the qualities of a good leader. (any three)

Effective leaders possess a combination of essential qualities that enable them to inspire, motivate, and guide their teams towards success. These qualities include:

  1. Visionary and Strategic Thinking: Leaders have a clear vision for the future and can develop strategic plans to achieve organizational goals.
  2. Effective Communication: Leaders communicate effectively, both verbally and in writing, to convey their vision, motivate their team, and build strong relationships.
  3. Decision-Making and Problem-Solving: Leaders can make sound decisions under pressure, analyze complex problems, and find creative solutions.
