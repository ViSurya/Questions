Write any five reasons of downfall of Maurya Empire.

The Maurya Empire's downfall can be attributed to several factors, including:

  1. Weak successors: After Ashoka's death, the empire faced a series of weak and incompetent rulers who lacked his vision and leadership.
  2. Economic problems: The empire faced economic challenges due to the high cost of maintaining a large army and bureaucracy, leading to increased taxes and discontent among the population.
  3. Internal conflicts: The empire was plagued by internal conflicts and power struggles among various factions, weakening its unity and stability.
  4. Foreign invasions: The empire faced invasions from various groups, including the Bactrian Greeks and the Shakas, who took advantage of its weakened state.
  5. Administrative issues: The empire's vast size and diverse population made it difficult to govern effectively, leading to administrative challenges and a lack of control over distant regions.
