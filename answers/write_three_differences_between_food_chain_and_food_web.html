Write three differences between food chain and food web.

Food Chain: A linear sequence of organisms through which nutrients and energy pass, with each organism consuming the one below it.

Food Web: A complex network of interconnected food chains, where multiple organisms consume and are consumed by various other organisms.

Key Differences: Food chains are linear, while food webs are complex networks. Food chains focus on a single path of energy transfer, while food webs consider multiple interconnected paths. Food chains are simpler and easier to understand, while food webs are more complex and realistic representations of ecological interactions.
