What are the advantages of 'Charkha', according to Gandhiji ? Write any Two.

According to Gandhiji, the 'Charkha' offered several advantages. Firstly, it was a symbol of self-reliance and economic independence, as it allowed individuals to produce their own cloth, reducing dependence on imported textiles. Secondly, it provided employment opportunities, particularly for women and marginalized communities, empowering them economically and socially.
