Mauryan Empire is the important age of Indian history, why ?

The Mauryan Empire holds immense significance in Indian history due to several reasons. It witnessed the establishment of a centralized administration, leading to political unity and stability across the vast Indian subcontinent. The empire's efficient governance, advanced economic policies, and promotion of trade and commerce contributed to its prosperity. Moreover, the Mauryan Empire played a crucial role in the spread of Buddhism, leaving a lasting impact on Indian culture and society.
